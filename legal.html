2. What we collect and why

a) Joining the early-access list

When you submit the form, we process the details you enter: your email address (required) and, optionally, your first name, the programme type you are interested in, and your country of residence. If you tick the optional box, we also record that you want to receive launch updates. To operate the double opt-in procedure and to evidence your consent, we also record the date and time and the IP address of your sign-up and of your email confirmation.

  • Purposes: to confirm your subscription (double opt-in); to send you the free Business School Application Playbook; to understand the early-access audience in aggregate (programme, country); and, only if you opt in, to send occasional launch updates including notifying you when we go live.
  • Legal basis: sending the requested guide and confirming your address — performance of your request and our legitimate interest, Art. 6(1)(b)/(f) GDPR; sending optional launch updates — your consent, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR; documenting consent — our legitimate interest in demonstrating compliance, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  • Data minimisation: only your email is required; first name, programme and country are optional. We do not collect nationality, ethnicity, or any special-category data (Art. 9 GDPR); country of residence is requested instead of nationality.
  • Withdrawal: you can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email or by contacting us. Withdrawal stops future processing and does not affect processing carried out before withdrawal.
  • Minimum age: you must be at least 16 to join the list (the age of valid consent in Germany). The prize draw additionally requires you to be 18 or over.

b) Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics (cookieless, EU-hosted) to understand how the site is used in aggregate. Plausible sets no cookies, stores no personal profiles, and does not track you across sites. Because it does not store or access information on your device and processes only aggregated, anonymous data, it does not require consent and runs for all visitors.

  • Legal basis: our legitimate interest in understanding aggregate site usage, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. See the Cookie Notice for details.

c) Server logs

Our hosting provider processes technical access logs (e.g. IP address, timestamp, requested page, browser type) as needed to deliver and secure the site — Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
